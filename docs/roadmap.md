# Our roadmap

## Visibility of our roadmap

Zephyr follows an agile development approach. The team continuously reviews progress, learns from outcomes, and adapts based on insights. Customer feedback plays a key role in shaping improvements and enhancing the overall product experience.

The public roadmap provides visibility into the key features planned for the next 12 to 18 months.

---

## Disclaimer

This roadmap includes information about upcoming features. The information is for reference only and should not be used for purchasing or planning decisions.

All roadmap items are subject to change or delay. Development, release, and timing depend on evolving priorities and requirements.

---

## Feature requests and ideas

Submit ideas to help improve Zephyr. Feedback helps prioritize features and identify valuable enhancements.

> **Note**  
> Submitted ideas are not visible immediately. The product management team reviews each submission and evaluates it against existing ideas. The team publishes only ideas that meet priority and feasibility criteria.
